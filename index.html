24/7 Consultation on a personal basis.

Our Pharmacy provides around the clock consultation with any drugs a customer is taking. This includes clearing up dosage amounts, drug therapy, and any questions one may have. At Kim Long, our patients are our priority and we are there for them every step of the way.

Advising on Medicare part D coverage.

With the rapid changes in Medicare, choosing a drug plan can be complicated. We are one of the few Pharmacies in the Houston area to offer free advising on obtaining part D coverage. With our help, a customer is ensured the best coverage possible for their needs.

Wide variety of hard to find medication.

The staff of Kim Long has always done everything to find medication that may not be easily obtained in the U.S. These may be the case with minority groups. As a result, we reach out to wholesalers and companies overseas to find medication that some of our customers are accustomed too.

Providing solutions to complex and diverse groups

Kim Long Pharamcy has been serving the greater Houston Area since 1984. We have over 25 years of experience in the industry and our team strives to offer our customers the best services possible. We distingush ourselves from other pharmacies by making the diverse community our focus. We take pride in being culturally senstive to the customer's wants and needs. By combining our industry experience with the personality and focus of a small buisness, we are able respond to the unique backgrounds of the Houston population.
